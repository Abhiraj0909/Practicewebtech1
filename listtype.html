  1. Raigad Fort is located in the Sahyadri mountain range of Raigad district in Maharashtra at an altitude of about 820 meters (2,700 ft) above sea level . It has a special identity in the history of Marathi Empire. Seeing the location and importance of Raigad, Chhatrapati Shivaji Raja made it the capital of his kingdom in the 16th century . Shiva Rajyabhishek took place at this place. After capturing the fort, the British looted and destroyed it. The fort is a protected monument of the Department of Archeology, Government of Maharashtra.
